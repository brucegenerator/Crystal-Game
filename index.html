Crystal Collector

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to you total score

You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above random number

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it

Each time the game starts the game will the values of each crystal
